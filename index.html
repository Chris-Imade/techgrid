About The Series

Transformative Technology Conversations

The Tech Grid Series is a forward-thinking knowledge exchange platform initiated by SurfSpot Communications Limited to explore the intersection of technology, innovation, and human development across Africa and beyond.

Designed as a recurring dialogue, the series convenes experts, industry leaders, innovators, and policymakers to engage in bold conversations around the most transformative technologies shaping our worldâ€”from Artificial Intelligence and cybersecurity to digital healthcare, education, broadband access, and the future of work.

How It Works

Series Structure

Themed Editions

Each edition of the Tech Grid Series is carefully curated around a specific theme, offering practical insights, emerging trends, and real-world case studies relevant to the African context.

Action-Oriented Format

The sessions are structured to be both informative and action-oriented, aiming to inspire strategic thinking, responsible innovation, and inclusive digital growth.

Our Vision

Human-Centred Technology Leadership

At the heart of the Tech Grid Series is a simple belief: technology must be human-centred, ethical, and inclusive. Whether exploring AI in healthcare or empowering digital careers, the series challenges conventional thinking and empowers audiences to navigate the digital future with purpose and preparedness.

Hosted and moderated by Engr. Akohamen Emmanuel Agenmonmen, Founder & CEO of SurfSpot Communications Ltd, each edition serves as a catalyst for collaboration, policy dialogue, and capacity-building within Africa's rapidly evolving tech ecosystem.
