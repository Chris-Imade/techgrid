Andrew Igbineweka

TEDx Speaker | Education Expert | Event Moderator

Andrew Igbineweka is a distinguished TEDx Speaker and education expert with almost a decade of experience in Learning Experience Design, Instructional Design, and Digital Learning. His passion lies in retooling professionals, educators, and organizations for the evolving career and business landscape of the 21st century.

Currently, Andrew serves as the Youth Engagement and Learning Officer at SkillzHQ, where he plays a pivotal role in developing innovative learning solutions that bridge the gap between traditional education and modern workforce requirements. His expertise in creating engaging, effective learning experiences has made him a sought-after professional in the education technology space.

In addition to his role at SkillzHQ, Andrew works as an Instructional Designer with World Merit, a global organization dedicated to empowering young leaders. In this capacity, he supports over 100,000 young people across 40+ countries who are actively advancing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. His work involves designing comprehensive learning programs that equip young leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to create meaningful impact in their communities.

Andrew is deeply passionate about bridging the gap between classroom education and workplace demands. Recognizing the disconnect that often exists between academic learning and practical application, he founded GoABitFurther Africa, a dynamic social learning community that provides professionals and students with opportunities for continuous learning and skill development.

As an entrepreneur and community builder, Andrew leads The Networking Salon, a unique global salon-style networking experience that connects professionals, business leaders, and brands across Africa, Europe, and the United States. This initiative reflects his commitment to fostering meaningful professional relationships and creating platforms for knowledge exchange and collaboration.

His expertise spans multiple areas including curriculum development, digital learning platforms, youth engagement strategies, and professional development programs. Andrew's approach to education is holistic, focusing not just on knowledge transfer but on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and adaptability skills that are essential in today's rapidly changing world.

As a TEDx Speaker, Andrew has shared his insights on education, youth empowerment, and the future of learning with diverse audiences. His speaking engagements focus on practical strategies for educational transformation and the importance of preparing learners for careers that may not yet exist.

Andrew's role as moderator for The Tech Grid Series brings together his expertise in facilitation, education, and technology. His ability to guide meaningful conversations and extract valuable insights from expert discussions makes him an ideal choice for moderating complex topics at the intersection of technology and human development.
